1. Introduction

1.1 About Getaways

Getaways is a comprehensive online marketplace platform that connects travelers with hosts offering accommodations, experiences, and services. The platform facilitates bookings, payments, communications, and reviews in a seamless, user-friendly environment.

1.2 Platform Overview

  • Accommodations: Book unique places to stay
  • Experiences: Discover and book local experiences
  • Services: Find and book various services
  • GetPay Wallet: Integrated e-wallet system for payments
  • Messaging System: Direct communication between guests and hosts
  • Review System: Rate and review bookings

1.3 User Roles

  • Guest: Browse, search, book, and review listings
  • Host: Create and manage listings, handle bookings, earn income
  • Admin: Manage platform operations, users, bookings, and policies

2. Getting Started

2.1 Account Creation

For Guests:

  1. Click “Sign Up” on the homepage
  2. Choose “I’m a Guest” account type
  3. Enter your email address and password
  4. Provide your first name and last name
  5. Accept terms and conditions
  6. Click “Create Account”
  7. Verify your email address (check your inbox)
  8. Click the verification link in the email

For Hosts:

  1. Click “Sign Up” on the homepage
  2. Choose “I’m a Host” account type
  3. Complete the registration form
  4. Verify your email address
  5. After verification, you’ll be prompted to select your hosting type:
    • Accommodation
    • Experience
    • Service
  6. Begin the onboarding process

Alternative: Google Sign-In

  • Click “Continue with Google”
  • Select your Google account
  • Choose your account type (Guest or Host)
  • Complete profile setup

2.2 Email Verification

  • Why it’s required: Email verification ensures account security and enables full platform access
  • How to verify:
    1. Check your email inbox after registration
    2. Click the verification link in the email
    3. You’ll be redirected to the platform
    4. If you didn’t receive the email, check spam folder or request a new verification email

2.3 Password Reset

  1. Click “Forgot Password” on the login page
  2. Enter your registered email address
  3. Check your email for reset instructions
  4. Click the reset link (valid for 1 hour)
  5. Enter your new password
  6. Confirm your new password
  7. Log in with your new password

2.4 Login

  1. Navigate to the login page
  2. Enter your email and password
  3. Optionally check “Remember Me” for persistent login
  4. Click “Log In”
  5. You’ll be redirected based on your role:
    • Guests → Guest Dashboard
    • Hosts → Host Dashboard
    • Admins → Admin Dashboard

  • Similar Listings: Recommendations based on your search

3.2 Making a Booking

Step 1: Select Dates and Guests

  1. Choose check-in and check-out dates
  2. Select number of guests
  3. Review pricing breakdown

Step 2: Review Booking Details

  • Total nights (for accommodations)
  • Base price
  • Service fees (if applicable)
  • Discounts (if coupon applied)
  • Total amount

Step 3: Apply Coupon (Optional)

  1. Enter coupon code in the booking form
  2. Click “Apply”
  3. Discount will be applied if valid
  4. Review updated pricing

Step 4: Choose Payment Method

  • GetPay Wallet: Pay immediately from wallet balance
  • PayPal: Pay via PayPal (payment processed when host confirms)
  • Pay Later: Reserve now, pay when host confirms

Step 5: Add Message to Host (Optional)

  • Include special requests or questions
  • This message will be sent via the messaging system

Step 6: Submit Booking Request

  1. Review all details
  2. Click “Request to Book”
  3. Booking status will be “Pending” until host responds
  4. You’ll receive email confirmation

3.3 Booking Management

Viewing Your Bookings

  1. Navigate to “Account Settings” → “Bookings” tab
  2. View all bookings with status:
    • Pending: Awaiting host confirmation
    • Confirmed: Host has accepted
    • Completed: Stay/service completed
    • Cancelled: Booking cancelled

Booking Details

  • Booking ID
  • Listing information
  • Dates and guests
  • Payment status
  • Host contact information
  • Cancellation policy

Payment for Confirmed Bookings

  • If you selected “Pay Later” or PayPal:
    1. Go to booking details
    2. Click “Complete Payment”
    3. Choose payment method
    4. Complete payment within 24 hours of host confirmation

Cancelling a Booking

  1. Open booking details
  2. Click “Cancel Booking”
  3. Review cancellation policy and refund amount
  4. Confirm cancellation
  5. Refund will be processed according to policy:
    • Flexible: Full refund if cancelled 24+ hours before check-in
    • Moderate: Full refund if cancelled 5+ days before check-in
    • Strict: 50% refund if cancelled 7+ days before check-in

3.4 Reviews and Ratings

Leaving a Review

  1. After booking completion, navigate to booking details
  2. Click “Write a Review”
  3. Rate from 1-5 stars
  4. Write your review comment
  5. Submit review
  6. Review will appear on the listing page

Review Guidelines

  • Be honest and constructive
  • Focus on your experience
  • Avoid personal information
  • Reviews cannot be edited after submission

3.5 Favorites/Wishlist

Managing Favorites

4. Host User Guide

4.1 Host Onboarding

Accommodation Onboarding Process

Step 1: Property Details - Select property type (House, Apartment, Condo, etc.) - Choose property structure - Select privacy type (Entire place, Private room, Shared room)

Step 2: Location - Enter complete address - Confirm location on map - Set location visibility (precise or approximate)

Step 3: Property Basics - Guest capacity - Number of bedrooms - Number of beds - Number of bathrooms

Step 4: Make It Stand Out - Add highlights (up to 5) - Select amenities - Upload photos (minimum 5 required) - Write title and description

Step 5: Pricing - Set weekday price - Set weekend price (optional) - Configure discounts: - Weekly discount - Monthly discount - Early bird discount - Last minute discount

Step 6: Booking Settings - Advance notice required - Preparation time - Availability window - Guest requirements - Instant booking (on/off)

Step 7: Final Details - House rules - Cancellation policy - Safety amenities

Step 8: Payment Setup - Connect PayPal account (for receiving payments) - Complete subscription payment: - Monthly: ₱999/month - Yearly: ₱9,999/year - Publish listing

Experience Onboarding Process

Step 1: Category Selection - Choose experience category - Select subcategory

Step 2: Location - Select where experience takes place - Enter meeting location

Step 3: Experience Details - Years of experience - Qualifications - Online profiles/portfolio - Residential address - Meeting address

Step 4: Experience Information - Upload photos - Create itinerary - Set maximum guests - Set price per guest

Step 5: Pricing and Availability - Review pricing - Set discounts - Configure availability

Step 6: Title and Description - Create compelling title - Write detailed description - Preview listing

Step 7: Payment and Publish - Complete subscription payment - Publish experience listing

Service Onboarding Process

Step 1: Category Selection - Choose service category

Step 2: Service Details - Location - Years of experience - Qualifications - Online profiles - Where service is provided - Service address

Step 3: Service Information - Upload photos - Service title - What you provide - Service description - Create offerings (if applicable)

Step 4: Offerings Setup (if applicable) - Offering title - Photos - Guest capacity - Pricing (per guest or fixed) - Discounts - Availability

Step 5: Payment and Publish - Complete subscription payment - Publish service listing

GetPay Wallet is the integrated e-wallet system for making and receiving payments on the platform.

Adding Funds (Cash In)

  2. Click “Cash In”

7. Troubleshooting

7.1 Common Issues

Login Problems

  • Forgot Password: Use password reset feature
  • Email Not Verified: Check email and verify account
  • Account Locked: Contact support

Booking Issues

  • Payment Failed: Check wallet balance or payment method
  • Booking Not Confirmed: Wait for host response (24-48 hours)
  • Cannot Cancel: Check cancellation policy and time limits

Payment Issues

  • Wallet Not Loading: Refresh page or clear cache
  • PayPal Not Working: Verify PayPal account connection
  • Refund Not Received: Contact support with booking ID

Messaging Issues

  • Messages Not Sending: Check internet connection
  • Not Receiving Messages: Check notification settings

7.2 Getting Help

Support Channels
